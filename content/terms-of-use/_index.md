Nom Zine is provided for learning, reference, and community discovery. Content may include links to external dictionaries, archives, fonts, and social spaces.

Use linked resources according to the terms of their original maintainers. Historical writing systems can involve uncertain readings and variants, so treat notes on this site as learning aids rather than final scholarly authority.
